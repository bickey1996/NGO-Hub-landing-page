Our Statistics

Our primary role is to make various NGOs accessible to anyone in the world. A yet another role is to enable NGOs collaborate under one roof so as to better help the needful. We measure our primary success in terms of help provided once requested. We measure our secondary success in terms of efficient donations made to other needful NGOs and actively monitoring their expenditure.

  • 11 Requested
  • 4 Helped
  • $55 Fund Recieved

  • 2 Active Operations

Transparency in our operations is very important for us. For our fellow NGOs, we have an active monitoring system to look into their expenditures. We try our best to get most accurate data presented here, however, it is not guarenteed. Data provided here may not be realtime but it does provide an insight of our operations.
